How we protect your data and privacy

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: Our Age Calculator operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information including:

  • Birth dates or personal dates you enter
  • Calculation results
  • IP addresses
  • Personal identifiers

All calculations are performed locally on your device and are not sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to use. Our service is designed to be completely private and anonymous.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: Any data you enter (such as dates) is stored only in your browser's local storage for convenience. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data
  • Is automatically deleted when you close your browser (unless you choose to save it)

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services that could collect anonymous usage data:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Google Fonts: Loads fonts from Google's servers

These services have their own privacy policies and may use cookies for analytics purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Language Preference: Remembers your language choice
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (if you accept them)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, you have complete control over your information:

  • No data to access, modify, or delete
  • No data to export
  • No data to restrict processing of
  • Complete anonymity guaranteed

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Any information stored locally in your browser is subject to your browser's data retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

9. International Data Transfers

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there are no international data transfers to be concerned about.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Last updated: January 2024

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our About Us page.
